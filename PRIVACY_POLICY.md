# Privacy Policy for Commission Engine

**Effective Date:** March 2026

At CodeProMax Tech, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the "Commission Engine" application for ERPNext.

## 1. Data Collection and Storage
The Commission Engine is a self-hosted or cloud-hosted open-source software application installed on your ERPNext instance. 
**We (CodeProMax Tech) do not collect, transmit, access, or store any of your data.**

All data processed by the Commission Engine—including but not limited to Sales Invoices, Commission Entries, Employee Data, Customer info, and Financial records—remains entirely on your own server or your Frappe Cloud infrastructure.

## 2. Telemetry and Tracking
The Commission Engine does not contain any tracking scripts, telemetry, phone-home mechanisms, or external analytics. It operates 100% within the closed ecosystem of your Frappe/ERPNext site.

## 3. Third-Party Services
If you choose to host the Commission Engine on Frappe Cloud or another hosting provider, your data is subject to the Privacy Policy and terms of that specific hosting provider.
